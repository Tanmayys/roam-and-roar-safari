6. Safari Rules & Guest Responsibilities

All guests must comply with the rules and regulations of Jim Corbett National Park as set by the Uttarakhand Forest Department. Key rules include:

  • Guests must carry valid government-issued photo ID on the day of the safari

  • Guests must not make loud noises, litter, or disturb wildlife
  • Feeding wildlife is strictly prohibited
