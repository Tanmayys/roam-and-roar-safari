2. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to process your safari booking inquiry. This may include:

  • Name — to address you and process your booking
  • Phone number — to contact you regarding your booking
  • Email address — to send booking confirmations and updates

  • Safari zone preference — to assist with the right booking

⚠️ Important: We do NOT collect Aadhaar numbers, PAN numbers, bank account details, credit/debit card information, or any other sensitive government or financial identification through our website inquiry forms. If government-issued ID is required for permit purposes, it will be requested separately and only at the time of confirmed booking, in accordance with government requirements.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your safari booking inquiry
  • To check permit and slot availability at Jim Corbett National Park
  • To send you booking confirmations and relevant trip information
  • To contact you for follow-up questions about your booking
  • To improve our services and customer experience

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing or sell it to third parties.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party. We may share limited information only in the following circumstances:

5. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. Your information is stored securely and accessed only by authorized team members involved in processing your booking.

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill your booking and comply with any applicable legal obligations.
